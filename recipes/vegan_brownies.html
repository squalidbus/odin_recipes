Vegan Brownies

Description

A very simple recipe for vegan brownies, with no nuts used! With 12 servings and ready to eat in 30-40 minutes, this is the perfect thing to make and share with friends.

The original recipe can be found on the Tesco website. I have made some slight changes.

Ingredients

  • Self-Raising Flour (150g)
  • Caster Sugar (125g)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dark Baking Chocolate (100g)
  • Margarine (75g)
  • Soya Milk (100ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1/2tsp)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven (gas 4, 180c, fan 160c).
  2. Grease a small baking tin (20cm x 20cm) with maragine.
  3. Mix the flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Break the dark chocolate into pieces and mix into a saucepan with the margarine over low heat.
  5. Once the chocolate and margarine have melted, add the soya milk and lemon juice. Stir to combine.
  6. Combine the chocolate mix with the dry ingredints. Stir until combined.
  7. Spread the mix into the brownie tin, and cook in the oven for 20-25 minutes.
