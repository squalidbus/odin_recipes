Vegan Brownies

Description

A very simple recipe for vegan brownies, with no nuts used! With 12 servings and ready to eat in 30-40 minutes, this is the perfect thing to make and share with friends.

The original recipe can be found on the Tesco website. I have made some slight changes.

Ingredients

  • Self-Raising Flour (150g)
  • Caster Sugar (125g)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Dark Baking Chocolate (100g)
  • Margarine (75g)
  • Soya Milk (100ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1/2tsp)
